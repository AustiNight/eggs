# PRODUCT.md — E.G.G.S.

**The Price of E.G.G.S.** (Explore, Gather, Group, Save) is an AI-powered grocery planning and price optimization tool built specifically for private event chefs and caterers. A chef inputs their menu and headcount; E.G.G.S. scales the ingredient list, finds the best prices across their preferred stores using official APIs and AI-assisted price discovery (no scraping), and produces a multi-store shopping plan with estimated costs — saving hours of manual comparison work per event and giving chefs a defensible cost basis for client pricing.

**Target user:** Private event chefs, personal chefs, and small caterers who shop for 10–100+ person events and need to optimize ingredient costs across multiple stores without spending hours on price research.

**Core value proposition:** Turn a menu into a multi-store shopping plan with cost estimates in minutes. Every event you log makes the next estimate more accurate. Know your food cost before you price the job.

**Why not household shoppers:** Household grocery comparison is a brutally competitive, low-willingness-to-pay market dominated by free apps. Private chefs have a clear ROI story ($100–240 saved per event vs. a $19/month subscription), a specific workflow need no current tool addresses, and are reachable through a focused community. This is the beachhead.
